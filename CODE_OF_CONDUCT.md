# Code of Conduct

Just-We is maintained as a practical engineering project. Participation should
stay focused, respectful, and useful to the people operating the software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Explain tradeoffs with technical evidence.
- Respect different experience levels and deployment contexts.
- Keep reviews focused on bugs, maintainability, security, and user impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, personal data, logs, tokens, QR sessions, or
  security reports without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests away from the project topic.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block contributors whose
behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
